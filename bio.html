Biography

Martin Niclose Netto is an Indian singer, song writer-composer and an onboard interactive performer with 4-octave vocal range... He got noticed by two of his works which is his own compositions and also cover music videos. Martin Netto is able to handle both Eastern and Western music such as Pop, Rock, Classic and Spanish folk songs.also Indian languages such as, Malayalam, Hindi, Tamil, and Telugu.

The multiple instruments he plays such as Guitar, ukulele and harmonica are self tough and learned from the internet. Young Martin struggled his childhood by the separation of his parents at the age of 10. He has grown, developed his skills without the support of parents. .

In his first song 'Believe in love' (song about a mother son affection)he claimed that the song he wrote is about his Mother in heaven. Also saying he considers mother Mary as his mother because he never experienced a mother's love until his age 18. Later discovered he has been adopted by mother Mary for many reasons.

The song 'Believe in love' is written at his age of 18 and published after four long years after his Engineering studies. In his lyrics he is expressing the bond between his mother and the way she raised him...

The boy who never believe in god at his young age later taught about God in church for 2 years...the reason he's saying is mother Mary changed his life and the way he used to see the world and showed him the true way and the light of life and love.

The second single You are a wonder is launched by the Cochin HRD. With the support of Rev Father Johny Xavier Puthukkad In the lyrics he is describing the love of his life whom he proposed at the age of 13. In the song he wrote how much she influenced In his life and what she means to him.

After his music videos get noticed he did many concerts by the name Martin netto live and also worked with famous play back singers such as Sidharth Menon, Nafla Sajid( who is his sister by heart-person who tough him vocal lessons and gave push to his career.

He has been invited as the guest performer in well known hotels like Crown plaza, Holiday Inn, Fragrant Nature, Radisson Blu and many more, also worked in an Indian cruise ship called Neffertiti.

Upcoming works are associated with Sri Vijay Babu (actor, producer) Also done deals with production companies for next originals and covers.
