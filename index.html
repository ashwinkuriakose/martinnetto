Biography

Martin Niclose Netto is an Indian singer, song writer-composer and an onboard interactive performer with 4-octave vocal range... He got noticed by two of his works which is his own compositions and also cover music videos.

Martin Netto is able to handle both Eastern and Western music such as Pop, Rock, Classic and Spanish folk songs.also Indian languages such as, Malayalam, Hindi, Tamil, and Telugu.
